Privacy Policy

BensBible — Effective February 22, 2026

BensBible is a personal Bible-reading app. Your privacy is important. This policy explains what information the app does and does not collect.

No Data Collection

BensBible does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. No account is required, and no data ever leaves your device.

Local Storage

Highlights, bookmarks, and notes you create are stored entirely on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is never uploaded to any server and is not accessible to the app developer.

Bible Text

The King James Version (KJV) text is bundled directly with the app. BensBible makes no network requests and does not fetch content from the internet.

Third-Party Services

BensBible does not include any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or other third-party SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is appropriate for all ages. Because no data is collected from anyone, there is no special handling required for users under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
