Frequently Asked Questions

How do I highlight a verse?

Tap and hold a verse to select it, then choose a highlight color from the menu that appears.

How do I add a note?

Tap the note icon next to any verse to open the note editor.

How do I bookmark a verse?

Tap and hold a verse, then select the bookmark option from the menu.

Is my data backed up?

Highlights, bookmarks, and notes are stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled in iOS Settings, this data is included in your device backup.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. The full KJV Bible text is bundled with the app and works completely offline.
